My story

I began my career drawn to patterns—how numbers reflect behavior, performance, and opportunity. What started as curiosity quickly became a practical skill: using data to ask better questions and support better decisions.

As I worked with reports and dashboards, I noticed a recurring challenge. Insights were often clear, but the tools around them were not. Teams struggled to access the right information at the right moment. That gap led me to full-stack development, where I could build systems that didn’t just analyze data, but delivered it directly into daily workflows.

Today, I work as a freelance Data and BI Analyst and Django developer, partnering with clients who want clarity and reliability from their data. I help transform raw information into insights—and insights into applications that support growth, efficiency, and confident decision-making.

My approach is calm and methodical: understand the problem, design with intention, and build solutions that are easy to trust and maintain.

I’ve always been drawn to the “why” behind performance—what drives growth, what slows teams down, and what decisions can be improved with evidence. That curiosity led me into data and business intelligence.

Over time, I noticed a pattern: insights often lived in spreadsheets or dashboards, but the day-to-day work happened elsewhere. So I leaned into full-stack development—building Django apps and APIs that bring data directly into workflows.

Today, as a freelancer, I help clients create trusted reporting, automate manual processes, and launch data-driven web products—so decisions are faster, clearer, and easier to repeat.
